Customer Spotlight

Adam Smith

As a connoisseur of the non-fiction genre, Adam gives insightful advice on a range of topics. He is a treasure trove of information, that always keeps fellow patrons engaged. He recommends us to get new books that increase the engagement of fellow customers, thus increasing our revenue.

Rebecca Herschel

Rebecca is a die-hard fiction fanatic. Specifically she loves her Fantasy novels. She always asks about our new selections and engages with staff and patrons about her favorite stories. She is a delight to be around, and we love to have her when she patronizes our store.

Robert Buchanan

Robert, or Bob as he likes to be called, loves his sports. He always peruses the sports magazines, and has the latest information and trivia about a wide variety of sports. We love when he engages with the fellow patrons about the latest and greats in sports trivia.

Community Involvement

School Book Fair

Every winter, we have a book fair for the elementary school students, to get them to enjoy the magical world of reading. We provide a cozy atmosphere, and help students find genres they might be interested in. We enjoy seeing their smiling faces, when they find that one book.

Charity Book Giveaway

Every few months, we give away a selection of our most popular books, for free, to the public. They can take as many as they want, and we hope through this effort, that it fosters a love reading within our community.
